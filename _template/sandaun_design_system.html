Brand palette

Colours come from the Sandaun provincial flag — a black upper triangle with white stars and a blue setting sun with gold rays, over a red lower triangle carrying the gold bird-of-paradise. “Sandaun” means sun-down: the province is the home of PNG’s sunset. Use the deep sun-blue for headings, gold for primary chart fills and KPI accents, red for emphasis and warnings, black sparingly for contrast.
